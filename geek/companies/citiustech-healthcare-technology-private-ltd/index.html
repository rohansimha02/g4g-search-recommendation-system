Intro and history: CitiusTech is a specialist provider of healthcare technology services and solutions to medical technology companies, providers, payers and life sciences organizations. Founded in 2005 by Rizwan Koita, CitiusTech has grown into a leading healthcare technology company with over 4,000 professionals worldwide. The company's unique offerings and execution excellence has enabled it to grow at an industry-leading pace, over the last five years. Industry: Healthcare Technology Product, Services and Operations: CitiusTech offers a wide range of services including healthcare software development, healthcare interoperability, regulatory compliance, BI/analytics, consumer engagement, care coordination and population health management. It also offers a suite of proprietary healthcare technology solutions like BI-Clinical, H-Scale, MIRA, and JIVA. Funding Details: In 2014, General Atlantic made a strategic investment in CitiusTech. The details of the funding are not publicly disclosed. Acquisitions: In 2019, CitiusTech acquired FluidEdge Consulting, a healthcare management consulting company in the US. Awards and Achievements: CitiusTech has been awarded the Best Companies to Work for in Healthcare IT by the Great Place to Work Institute and has been recognized as a Leader in Everest Group’s Healthcare IT Services PEAK Matrix™ Assessment. Note: The above information is sourced from various online resources and the official website of CitiusTech.
