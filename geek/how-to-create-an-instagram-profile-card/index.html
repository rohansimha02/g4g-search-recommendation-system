Instagram has recently introduced a new feature called Instagram Profile Card, a convenient and visually appealing way to share your Instagram handle with others. It's a scannable QR code that leads directly to your Instagram profile. You can use it in various ways, such as sharing it online, printing it on business cards, or including it in promotional materials to make it easier for people to find and follow you.

In this guide, we'll walk you through how to create an Instagram Profile Card and how to use it effectively.

Table of Content

  • What is an Instagram Profile Card?
  • How to Create an Instagram Profile Card
  • How to Use Instagram Profile Cards Effectively
  • How to Share your new Instagram Profile Card?
  • Best Practices for Using Instagram Profile Cards

What is an Instagram Profile Card?

An Instagram Profile Card is a feature provided by Instagram that allows you to generate a scannable QR code linked to your profile. When someone scans the QR code using their smartphone, it takes them directly to your Instagram profile, making it easy for them to follow you. This can be particularly useful for businesses, influencers, content creators, or anyone who wants to grow their social media presence.

An Instagram Profile Card is essentially a QR code generated by Instagram that links directly to your profile. This card can be shared in real life (on printed materials like business cards or posters) or digitally (via social media, emails, or websites). When someone scans the QR code using their phone, it takes them directly to your Instagram profile.

How to Create an Instagram Profile Card

Creating an Instagram Profile Card is simple and can be done directly from the Instagram app. Follow these steps to create your Profile Card:

Step 1: Open the Instagram App

  • Launch the Instagram app on your mobile device.
  • Make sure you are logged in to the account for which you want to create the Profile Card.

Step 2: Go to Your Profile

  • Tap on your profile icon in the bottom right corner to navigate to your profile page.

Step 3: Access the QR Code Option

  • Tap on the three horizontal lines (menu icon) in the top right corner.
  • Select “QR Code” from the menu options. This will open your Instagram Profile Card.

Step 4: Customize Your Profile Card

  • Instagram allows you to customize the appearance of your Profile Card by changing the background color, adding emojis, or using a selfie.
  • Swipe left or right to choose different background options:
  1. Color: Tap the screen to cycle through different background colors.
  2. Emoji: Choose from a variety of emojis to set as your background.
  3. Selfie: Use your camera to take a selfie, and Instagram will add fun effects to it.

Step 5: Save or Share Your Instagram Profile Card

  • Once you have customized your Profile Card, you can save it to your device by taking a screenshot, or you can share it directly by tapping the share icon.
  • You can also print the QR code, add it to your website, or share it on other social media platforms.

Where to Get Instagram Profile Card in Instagram Mobile App?

To access your Instagram Profile Card in the Instagram mobile app, you can:

Step 1: Go to your profile

Step 2: Select Share Profile

Step 3: Your profile card will appear automatically

How to Use Instagram Profile Cards Effectively

Now that you have created your Instagram Profile Card, there are several ways you can use it to boost your social media presence and engage your audience:

1. Share It Online

  • Post your Instagram Profile Card on other social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, or even Snapchat.
  • Add it to your email signature to make it easy for email recipients to follow you on Instagram.
  • Include the QR code on your website or blog. You can add it to your contact page or any other relevant section where you promote your social media profiles.

2. Print It on Business Cards and Flyers

  • Include the QR code on your business cards, flyers, brochures, or any promotional material. This is especially helpful for businesses that want to attract more followers from in-person events.
  • If you’re a speaker at an event or participating in a trade show, include the Profile Card in your presentation slides or booth displays.

3. Use It in Your Physical Store

  • If you have a physical store or location, place your Instagram Profile Card at the checkout counter, on the door, or near promotional displays.
  • This encourages customers to follow you on Instagram, where you can keep them updated with the latest offers, events, or new arrivals.

4. Include It in Event Materials

  • For event organizers, adding an Instagram Profile Card to event programs, name tags, or banners makes it easier for attendees to find and follow the event's official Instagram account.
  • You can also print the Profile Card on event tickets to encourage people to follow your Instagram account even before they attend.

5. Add It to Presentations and Digital Content

  • If you are giving a presentation or hosting a webinar, include the Instagram Profile Card on one of your slides so that attendees can easily scan and follow your profile.
  • Use it in digital downloads, like eBooks or whitepapers, to make it easy for readers to connect with you on Instagram.

6. Use It for Networking

  • When attending networking events, you can share your Instagram Profile Card with other attendees to grow your network. It’s a great way to stay connected and expand your audience.

How to Share your new Instagram Profile Card?

Here’s a step-by-step guide to sharing your new Instagram profile card:

Step 1: Open the App:

Launch the Instagram app on your mobile device.

Step 2: Go to Your Profile:

Tap your profile picture icon located at the bottom right of the screen to go to your profile page.

Step 3: Access the Menu:

Tap the three horizontal lines (menu icon) in the top right corner of your profile page.

Step 4: Select QR Code:

From the menu, choose the "QR Code" option. This will display your personal Instagram QR code, which acts as your profile card.

Step 5: Share Your Profile Card:

You can now share your profile card in two ways: Direct Share or Download and Share.

Step 6: Direct Share:

Tap the 'Share' icon (usually at the top right of the screen) to send your QR code directly via other apps like messaging or email.

Step 7: Download:

Tap 'Save Image' to download the QR code to your device, which you can then share as an image file anytime.

By following these steps, you can easily share your Instagram profile card with others, allowing them to quickly scan and follow your profile.

Best Practices for Using Instagram Profile Cards

To make the most out of your Instagram Profile Card, follow these best practices:

1. Place It Where It’s Visible and Accessible

Make sure your Profile Card is placed in an area where people can easily see and scan it. For example, if you are placing it in a store, position it near the entrance or checkout counter.

2. Test the QR Code Before Sharing

Always test the QR code before sharing it to ensure that it works correctly and directs users to your Instagram profile.

3. Use High-Quality Prints for Physical Materials

If you’re printing the QR code on business cards, flyers, or posters, ensure that the print quality is high enough to make the QR code easily scannable.

4. Incorporate a Call to Action

Add a call to action (CTA) next to your Instagram Profile Card, such as “Follow us on Instagram for the latest updates!” or “Scan to follow!”. This encourages people to take action and helps increase your follower count.

5. Keep It Updated

If you change your Instagram handle or make significant changes to your profile, update the QR code accordingly to avoid directing users to an incorrect or broken link.

Conclusion

The Instagram Profile Card is a convenient and effective tool to share your profile across different platforms, both online and offline. Whether you're an individual trying to grow your personal brand or a business aiming to connect with more customers, the Profile Card offers an easy way for people to follow you with just a scan. Start using your Instagram Profile Card today to reach a larger audience and enhance your digital presence!

How to Create Instagram Profile Card - FAQs

1. Can I customize the Instagram Profile Card?

Yes, you can customize the background of your Instagram Profile Card with colors, emojis, or even a selfie. Instagram provides several options for customization.

2. Can I create multiple Instagram Profile Cards for different purposes?

You can customize your Profile Card for different occasions, but Instagram only allows you to have one QR code that links to your profile. You can change the appearance as often as needed.

3. Do Instagram Profile Cards expire?

No, Instagram Profile Cards do not expire. As long as your Instagram profile is active, the QR code will remain valid.

4. Can I track the number of scans my Instagram Profile Card gets?

Instagram does not provide built-in tracking for QR code scans. However, you can use third-party services to create a trackable QR code if you need analytics.

5. How do I share my Instagram Profile Card online?

You can save your Profile Card by taking a screenshot or sharing it directly from the Instagram app. Post it on social media, add it to your email signature, or use it on your website to share it online.
